Our Products

Feel the taste of coffee with our products. We offer high quality and tasty coffee variations from widely known brands.

Gevalia Barista Dark Roast Espresso

Experience a truly unique coffee bean. Gevalia Barista Dark Espresso is a dark roasted coffee with strength and dark and intense taste. The coffee consists of 100% Arabica coffee beans that have been roasted slowly and gently to release aromas.

Espresso Intenso

Here you have a Espresso mix, made from dark tined Arabica and Robusta beans. The result is a cup with a strong and intense shape with spicy and pleasant aftertaste. It serve well as the basis for one of the many Espresso based cofe classics, such as Cappuccino or Latte Macchiatto.

Crema Aroma

Crema Aroma is a blend of Arabica beans from Central and South America and Robusta beans from Africa. The combination gives an intense taste experience with a delicate bitterness that rounds off the coffee perfectly. The beans are dark roasted for an espresso or as a base for cappuccino.
